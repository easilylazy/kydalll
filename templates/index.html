Clouds, Ice, and Bounty: The Lee and Juliet Folger Fund Collection of Seventeenth-Century Dutch and Flemish Paintings

This exhibition brings together a rich cross-section of 17th-century Dutch and Flemish life and culture offering a unique opportunity to enjoy some of the finest productions of the period.

Aquatint: From Its Origins to Goya

In the second half of the eighteenth century, the blossoming new printmaking techniqueaquatintvastly expanded possibilities for creating and disseminating images. Most of the works in this exhibition have never been on public view. 

Conversations: Carrie Mae Weems and The Shaw 54th Regiment Memorial

Come and experience how Carrie Mae Weems responds to The Shaw 54th Regiment Memorial, created a century earlier to honor one of the first Civil War regiments of African Americans formed in the North.
